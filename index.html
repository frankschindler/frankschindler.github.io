Welcome!

I'm Frank, currently a fourth year PhD student in the condensed matter theory group at the physics department of the University of Zurich.

In my research I study novel states of quantum matter, so called topological phases. I also have a standing interest in applying machine-learning techniques to condensed matter physics.

You can find my publications on the arXiv, publons, and Google Scholar.
