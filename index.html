Frank Schindler

Hi! I'm a lecturer in condensed matter theory at Imperial College London.

My research is on topological phases of matter: their theoretical underpinnings, their physical consequences, what they can be used for, and how they can be generalized.

You can find my publications on my college webpage, arXiv, and Scholar.
