Hi!

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Princeton Center for Theoretical Science, working on theoretical condensed matter physics. Before that, I was a PhD student in the condensed matter theory group at the University of Zurich in Switzerland.

In my research I study novel states of quantum matter, so-called topological phases. I also have a long-standing interest in applying machine-learning techniques to condensed matter physics.

You can find my publications here, as well as on the arXiv and Google Scholar.
