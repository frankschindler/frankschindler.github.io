Frank Schindler

Hi! I'm an assistant professor in condensed matter theory at Imperial College London.

My research is on topological phases of matter, and more generally on topological phenomena in quantum materials, metamaterials, and quantum dynamics.

You can find my publications on arXiv and Google Scholar.
