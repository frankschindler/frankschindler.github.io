Hi!

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Princeton Center for Theoretical Science, working on theoretical condensed matter physics. Before, I was a PhD student in the condensed matter theory group at the University of Zurich.

My research focuses on topological phases of matter, strongly correlated materials, quantum thermalization, and their intersection. You can find my publications here, as well as on arXiv, Google Scholar, ORCID, and Publons.
